Cake Pops

With this recipe, you can adapt it to create whatever flavors and designs fit your purposes. Using boxed cake mixes and frosting makes this a quick and easy bake that tastes amazing and is sure to wow anyone who tries them.

Ingredients:

1 (15.25oz) package cake mix

1 (16 oz) container frosting

1 bag candy melts (more if other colors desired*)

*I found Wilton candy melts to be easiest to work with

Directions

Step 1: Prepare the cake as directed on the box.

Step 2: While cake is still warm, cut off and remove the edges (this will prevent large crumbs in the balls). Crumble remaining cake in a large mixing bowl.

Step 3: Add frosting to warm cake until it reaches a moist consistency that holds its shape. (I use only about 3/4 of the jar of frosting)

Step 4: Roll cake mixture into desired size balls and freeze on baking sheet for at least one hour.

Step 5: Decorating! This is mostly up to you. Follow the directions on the bag of candy melts for best results. I found that using 1-2 Tbsp of shortening helped to keep the coating from thickening too quickly while decorating.

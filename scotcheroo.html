Scotcheroo Balls

Every time I go home, my family begs me to make a batch of these Scotcheroo Balls. If you have ever had Scotcheroo bars, this is a tasty spin on that recipe that is a lot easier to make and a definite crowd pleaser. This recipe makes about 18-20 balls, but if your family's anything like mine, you'll be doubling the recipe in no time.

Ingredients:

1 C creamy peanut butter

1/4 C butter, softened

1 C powdered sugar

2 C Rice Krispies

1 C butterscotch chips

1/2 C semi-sweet chocolate chips

1/2 C dark chocolate chips

2 T coconut oil, not liquid

Directions

Step 1: Line cookie sheet with parchment or wax paper.

Step 2: Beat peanut butter, softened butter, and powdered sugar until smooth.

Step 3: Chill mixture in freezer for up to one hour to reduce stickiness while rolling balls.

Step 4: Roll mixture into balls and chill on lined cookie sheet for 30 minutes.

Step 5: In microwaveable bowl, add chocolate chips and coconut oil. Microwave in 30 second intervals, stirring in-between until melted.

Step 6: Dip or cover balls in chocolate mixture.

Step 7: Refrigerate in container and enjoy for up to two weeks.
